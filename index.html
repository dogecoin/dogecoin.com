CHOOSE AN ONLINE WALLET:

Online wallets are the quickest and easiest way to use Dogecoin, but lack the security of storing your wallet on your local computer. We recommend you only store a small amount of Dogecoin in an online wallet at any time. There are several secure, browser-based wallets available to choose from:

  • DogeVault
  • DogeChain Wallet
  • DogeAPI
  • Moolah
  • HolyTransaction

Note: These online wallets are not owned or maintained by Dogecoin or the Dogecoin Foundation. While we are satisfied they are trustworthy, reliable services - please use at your own risk.
