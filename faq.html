Dogecoin Currency

What is Dogecoin?

Dogecoin is a decentralised cryptocurrency based on the doge meme.

What do you mean decentralised?

Dogecoin is not run by any individual company or person. Technically speaking, it operates according to rules agreed by consensus, rather than decided upon by some founding or owning entity.

What do you mean cryptocurrency?

A cryptocurrency is a digital currency that is secured by cryptography, in constrast to conventional (fiat) currencies where they are secured by a government or governments.

Okay but what does that mean?

It means you don't have to trust any central organisation or person the way you would a bank, but also that you have to be your own bank. The developers can't seize your funds, but also can't return them if you send them to the wrong address.
