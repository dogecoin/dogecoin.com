Why Dogecoin

Dogecoin is an open source project, with one of the largest crypto communities, favored by Shiba Inus worldwide.

Wide Acceptance Rate

Dogecoin, unlike many other coins, has one of the largest acceptance rates. In fact, recently, the Dallas Mavericks (USA Baseball Team) had over 20,000 Dogecoin Transactions!

Eco-Frendly

Dogecoin is one of the most eco-frendly coin to mine (at 1 coin per minute). This means, when you mine your doge, you are using significanly less fossil fuels than other coins like Bitcoin.

Community

Dogecoin has one of the largest communities, from Reddit to Discord Community there are Dogecoin supporters like you everywhere.

Simple And Easy To use

Dogecoin has a very low transaction fee, as well clean transactions numbers. When you use most coins, you have to send it in numbers like .0008, whereas Dogecoin, you can send it in clean numbers like 69.

Develop

Dogecoin is free software. If you are a software developer, you can use your shibapowers to do good and improve Dogecoin. You can even build amazing new services or software that use Dogecoin.
